In this scenario, the greatest after-tax value is that of the Jacksonville Jaguars with a value of $73,805,923, which is $5,538,232 greater than that of Washington's $69,251,831 after tax value. Arizona also has a slight tax advantage over Washington, resulting in $984,140 more net income. New York, Los Angeles, and San Francisco all have higher tax burdens resulting in less net income. While New York's is a modest $2,224,164 less than Washington, the difference starts to become real if playing for San Francisco or Los Angeles, as the net income difference exceeds $5,000,000.
